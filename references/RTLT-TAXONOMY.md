# FEMA RTLT Full Taxonomy Reference
## Source: rtlt.preptoolkit.fema.gov (612 entries as of April 2026)

The RTLT contains four entry types:
1. **Resource Typing Definitions** — equipment, teams, units (ID prefix: X-508-XXXX)
2. **Position Qualifications** — personnel credentials (ID prefix: X-509-XXXX)
3. **Position Task Books (PTBs)** — competency completion records (ID prefix: PTB-XXXX)
4. **EOC Skillsets** — modular qualifications for EOC personnel

## Core Capabilities Covered by RTLT (FEMA's 32 total; RTLT covers these)

### Response Mission Area
1. Operational Coordination (IMT, ICS positions, EOC)
2. Operational Communications (COML, COMT, AUXCOMM)
3. Mass Care Services (Shelter, Feeding, AFN, Animal Response)
4. Mass Search and Rescue Operations (US&R, SWFRT, Land SAR, Water SAR, Air SAR)
5. Fire Management and Suppression (Structural, Wildland, ARFF, HazMat)
6. Public Health, Healthcare, and Emergency Medical Services (EMS, Medical, Public Health)
7. On-scene Security, Protection, and Law Enforcement (SWAT, EOD, LE)
8. Fatality Management Services (DMORT, ME, Victim ID)
9. Infrastructure Systems (Public Works, Utilities, Damage Assessment)
10. Environmental Response/Health and Safety
11. Logistics and Supply Chain Management
12. Situational Assessment

### Cross-cutting
13. Planning
14. Public Information and Warning
15. Cybersecurity

## Grey Sky Scope Decision (Roy, 2026-04-09)

**Grey Sky will build certification pathways for EVERY position and team in the RTLT database — not just the 12 SRT disciplines.**

This means Grey Sky covers:
- All ICS/IMT positions (from IC to Unit Leaders)
- All EOC positions and skillsets
- All medical/public health positions
- All law enforcement/tactical positions
- All SAR disciplines
- All mass care/shelter positions
- All public works/infrastructure positions
- All communications positions
- All logistics positions
- All fatality management positions
- All environmental response positions
- All cybersecurity positions
- Every position, team, and resource type FEMA defines

**Grey Sky doesn't just serve "first responders" — it serves EVERYONE who supports disaster operations.**
